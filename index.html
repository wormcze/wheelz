# Privacy Policy for Wheelz Last Updated: April 29, 2026 Greedy Goblin Studio ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and all users of our mobile game, Wheelz. 1. No Data CollectionWheelz does not collect, store, or share any personal information from its users. We do not collect names, email addresses, precise location data, device identifiers, or any other personal data. 2. No Third-Party Advertising or TrackingThe app does not contain any third-party advertising or behavioral tracking. We do not use any third-party SDKs that fingerprint or track users. 3. Made for Kids & Parental GateWheelz is designed for children aged 6–8. To ensure safety, all external links and in-app purchases are protected by a parental math challenge to prevent children from accessing them without a grown-up. 4. Offline PlayWheelz is fully playable offline and does not require an internet connection to access its core gameplay features. 5. In-App PurchasesThe app offers a single, non-consumable in-app purchase that unlocks all content. This purchase is processed through Apple’s secure payment system and is gated by our parental math challenge. 6.
