Privacy Policy for Wheelz

Last Updated: April 29, 2026

Greedy Goblin Studio ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and all users of our mobile game, Wheelz.

1. No Data Collection

Wheelz does not collect, store, or share any personal information from its users. We do not collect names, email addresses, precise location data, device identifiers, or any other personal data.

2. No Third-Party Advertising or Tracking

The app does not contain any third-party advertising or behavioral tracking. We do not use any third-party SDKs that fingerprint or track users.

3. Made for Kids & Parental Gate

Wheelz is designed for children aged 6–8. To ensure safety, all external links and in-app purchases are protected by a parental math challenge to prevent children from accessing them without a grown-up.

4. Offline Play

Wheelz is fully playable offline and does not require an internet connection to access its core gameplay features.

5. In-App Purchases

The app offers a single, non-consumable in-app purchase that unlocks all content. This purchase is processed through Apple’s secure payment system and is gated by our parental math challenge.
