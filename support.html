Common Questions

How do I restore my purchase?

Wheelz features a "Restore Purchases" button located within the parental gate. If you have previously purchased the premium unlock, simply enter the parental gate and tap "Restore" while signed into the same Apple ID.

Does the app work without internet?

Yes. Wheelz is designed to be fully playable offline, making it perfect for travel.

Is the app safe for kids?

Absolutely. Wheelz is built for children aged 6–8. We do not show third-party ads, we do not track user behavior, and all external links or purchases are protected by a parental math challenge.
